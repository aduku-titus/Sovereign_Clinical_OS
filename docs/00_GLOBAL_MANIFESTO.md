# THE MANIFESTO: MEDICAL SOVEREIGNTY
In 2026, data is the new border. We believe:
1. **Clinical Autonomy:** A hospital that cannot function without an internet connection is a liability.
2. **Privacy as a Default:** PII should never leave the physical building.
3. **Deterministic Safety:** AI "hallucinations" in medicine are not bugs; they are safety violations. We use a hard-coded logic shield.
