# Compliance: EU GDPR & AI ACT
**Focus:** High-Risk AI Governance

## Alignment Overview
Designed to meet the rigorous standards of the **EU AI Act** (for High-Risk Medical Devices) and **GDPR Article 32**.

## Key Statutory Alignments
1. **High-Risk Transparency:** The **Litigation Shield** provides the required logging and transparency for AI systems used in healthcare triage.
2. **GDPR Article 32 (Security):** Implements encryption and pseudonymization as native features of the **Sovereign Vault**.
3. **Human Oversight:** The **LangGraph State Management** ensures that the AI cannot transition to a "Discharged" state without a human "Sign-off" event.
